1. Resilience and Grit (Overcoming Adversity)
These quotes emphasize persisting through difficult times, learning from failure, and having the courage to keep going. 
"If you are going through hell, keep going." — Winston Churchill
"To fall seven times, to get up eight, life starts from now." — Japanese Proverb
"Every strike brings me closer to the next home run." — Babe Ruth
"You may not be sure, whether the storm is really over. But... you won't be the same person who walked in." — Haruki Murakami

"Why not go out on a limb? That's where the fruit is." — Mark Twain
"Setting goals is the first step in turning the invisible into the visible." — Tony Robbins
"You miss 100% of the shots you don't take." — Wayne Gretzky

3. Discipline and Perseverance (Hard Work)
These highlight that long-term success comes from consistent effort, focus, and grit rather than talent alone.
"Nothing in the world can take the place of Perseverance. Talent will not... Genius will not... Education will not..." — Calvin Coolidge
"The successful warrior is the average man, with laser-like focus." — Bruce Lee
"If I had eight hours to chop down a tree, I'd spend six hours sharpening my ax." — Abraham Lincoln 
4. Cognitive Reframing (Positive Thinking/Mindset)
These quotes focus on controlling your attitude, changing your perspective on problems, and cultivating mental strength.
"The happiness of your life depends on the quality of your thoughts." — Marcus Aurelius
"Doubt kills more dreams than failure ever will." — Suzy Kassem
"Once you replace negative thoughts with positive ones, you'll start having positive results." — Willie Nelson
"Don't let a bad day make you think that you have a bad life." — Unknown

5. Self-Reliance and Personal Empowerment
These focus on believing in yourself, taking control of your destiny, and not waiting for permission or help. 
"Only I can change my life. No one can do it for me." — Carol Burnett
"The healer you have been looking for is your own courage to know and love yourself completely." — Yung Pueblo
"Believe you can and you're halfway there." — Theodore Roosevelt 
6. Perspective on Success and Life (Value-Based)
These define success through impact, character, and enjoyment rather than monetary gain or fame. 
"Strive not to be a success, but rather to be of value." — Albert Einstein
"Life is about making an impact, not making an income." — Kevin Kruse
"Success is not the key to happiness. Happiness is the key to success." — Albert Schweitzer
